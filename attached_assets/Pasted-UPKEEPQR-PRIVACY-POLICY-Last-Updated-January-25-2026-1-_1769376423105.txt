UPKEEPQR PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: January 25, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how UpKeepQR, LLC ("UpKeepQR", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and discloses your personal information in the course of our business operations.

"Personal Data" means any data relating to an identified or identifiable natural person that is processed by UpKeepQR as described in this Policy when such information is protected as "personal data" or "personal information" under applicable data protection laws.

"Services" means all of UpKeepQR's websites, applications (including mobile and web applications), QR code-based home maintenance management tools, platforms, and services offered by UpKeepQR.

"Website" means the UpKeepQR.com website or any other proprietary website owned by UpKeepQR or that UpKeepQR has the authority to manage and control and which link to this Policy.

2. APPLICABILITY

This Privacy Policy applies to Personal Data for which UpKeepQR acts as a controller, such as when you:
- Visit our Website
- Create a homeowner account
- Purchase QR code magnets
- Use our home maintenance management services
- Subscribe to SMS reminders
- Contact us for support

When our customers (homeowners) use our Services to manage their home maintenance data, UpKeepQR acts as a controller of that data and processes it in accordance with this Policy and our Terms of Service.

3. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

3.1 Account Information

When you create an account with UpKeepQR, we collect:

- Email address

If you purchase QR code magnets or subscribe to premium services, our third-party payment processor (Stripe) collects and stores:

3.5 Automatically Collected Information

When you access our Website or Services, we automatically collect:

- Browser type and version

- Pages visited and features used

3.6 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, pixels, web beacons, and similar technologies to:

- Remember your preferences
- Analyze usage patterns
- Improve our Services
- Detect and prevent fraud

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie management banner.

3.7 SMS and Communication Data

If you opt in to SMS reminders:

- SMS delivery status
- Message engagement (delivered, opened)
- Opt-in/opt-out preferences

3.8 Usage Analytics

We collect information about how you use our Services:
- Features most frequently used
- Maintenance tasks completed
- QR code scans
- Time spent on platform
- App interactions
- Error reports and crash data

We take the security of your Personal Data seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect it:

- Secure authentication mechanisms

However, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to:

7. DATA RETENTION

We retain your Personal Data for as long as:
- Your account is active
- Necessary to provide our Services
- Required to comply with legal obligations
- Needed to resolve disputes or enforce our agreements

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your Personal Data within 90 days, except where we must retain it for legal or regulatory purposes.

Specifically:
- Account data: Retained while account is active
- Maintenance records: Retained for 7 years (typical home maintenance cycle)
- Payment records: Retained for 7 years (tax and legal requirements)
- Marketing data: Retained until you opt out, then deleted within 30 days
- Support tickets: Retained for 3 years
